PLUMBING

Our reputation for quality, service, and customer satisfaction have made plumbing one of the main services we offer for over 19 years. No matter what your plumbing needs are, we can handle it, from a simple fix to a complete bathroom or kitchen design. Tradesmen from our hand-picked team can carry out complete renovations or part renovations, specialising in their trades to provide an exclusive finish.

BOILER & HEATING INSTALLATIONS

As Gas Safe Registered heating engineers, we provide a complete range of heating & boiler services, including installation of new boilers, as well as fault finding and diagnostic services. As a gas-safe registered company, Veepo Plumbing Solutions Ltd can also provide boiler services in Enfield, Harrow, and other parts of North London and North West London.

RENOVATIONS AND NEWBUILD

Our builders are qualified and experienced tradesmen who specialise in new builds, renovations, and refurbishments. Whether you have existing plans or want to create something new, we can build interiors and exteriors to your specifications while we are knowledgeable about the requirements of period buildings. Our services include construction services such as facing brick repairs, pointing, and building. Our highly skilled plasterers can perform rendering and plastering to a high standard regardless of whether it is for a new construction or a renovation. Please contact us at your earliest convenience for a quote on your needs.

COMMERCIAL PROPERTY

We have had an increasing number of enquiries for commercial services and as a result, we now undertake work across commercial heating, electrics and drains etc. Our commercially qualified team and electricians have extensive experience in the commercial sector. We can provide you with a solution, from a plumbing related problem to the design and creation of new office space. Whether you are looking for a complete or partial refurbishment, our tradesmen are experts in their trades and can provide you with an exclusive finish.

ROOFING & GUTTERS

Our team are experienced professionals in renewing or repairing all types of roofs and can undertake everything from a small leak to a completely new roof with the utmost care and competence. Our roofers are highly skilled, fully qualified, and fully insured in the unlikely case of accidents. We will repair, clean or fit guttering, downpipes, fascias and soffits and can fully insulate all types of roof, including pitched and flat roofs, in accordance with Part L of the building regulations. We will always ensure minimum disruption and will always leave your property clean and tidy following their visit.

FLOORING & CARPENTRY

Our carpenters are experienced, craftsmen at the top of their trade. By using the best tradesmen in the industry our reputation has been established as the go-to company when quality and service matters. Our carpenters also provide emergency services for such things as doors, windows or accidental damage, ensuring minimum disruption and leaving your premises clean and tidy following their visit. We can even provide a team of multi-skilled tradesmen for very large projects, with each member being a specialist within their own trade.
